School Life

The School Church

Was built in 1930

Our school provides opportunities for students, staff and parents to know and celebrate our Catholic faith. This is done through the religious life of our school. Members of our community experience a rich liturgical and prayer life, celebration of the Sacraments, faith formation, social outreach activities, and opportunities to share faith with each other and with youth from other schools in the Archdiocese in our Church



The School Library

Provides 24/7 Access To a Wealth

Our library provides a social space to meet, collaborate, research, learn, share, and relax. The staff work to develop a reading culture and raise the levels of information literacy across the school by leading students through processes which empower them to be critical thinkers, enthusiastic readers and skillful, efficient and discerning researchers.



The Multi Purpose Hall (MPH)

  • Implemented Online Attandece Management System

    1. Accuracy
    Humans are prone to error, even if we provide them with supporting computational devices. Automated attendance management systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the inevitable and costly errors with annual data entry. This accurate data therby help to provide accurate performance and playroll data.

    2. Eccnomies
    Have you ever thought that attendance management systems could control costs? It saves money by putting an end to inaccurate time reporting, buddy punching, absenteeism, tardiness, time abuse, and overpayment. As a matter of fact, a study done by the Assuim Group found that large organizations save up to $1,600 per employee after implementing automated attendance management systems.

    3. Productivity/Efficiency
    Monitering and managing attendance usually can be time comsuming, labourous, and expensive affiar. It takes time to to process paper sheets and time cards, create schedules, authorise leave and overtime, and create mantually. Free up precious adminstration time with automated system that does it all for you, from keeping track of employees hours to automaticially importing imformation into your payroll system. The time and effort saved combined with data accuracy helps in optimiring the use of resorces which lead to increased productivity and improves profits.
